Spreading Paw-sitivity:
You're one call away from saving a life

Petition by
Fur & Tails
{#

{# #}

There is a fine line between human being and being human. Every year more than a thousand of animals (especially Dogs) go crazy and get lost & injured in our festival of lights. Every life matters and it really costs nothing to save a life at Furandtails.in

Our community comes out with a vision to save those voiceless souls who are suffering day in and dayout. This Diwali we would not request people just to stop bursting crackers. We will talk about something more realistic and bigger. We want you to be a hero yourself and take an initiative towards helping and rescuing Dogs. Sign this Petition not only to promote less crackers but also to rescue hurt, lost and injured Dogs. All you need to do is to drop us a message and provide us the details of the rescue scenario. We will always be just a call away. We will provide free medical assistance and reallocation for the lost dogs. Moreover, We will update you once the dog reported by you is rescued. Anyone who saves a life would get a batch of honour from Fur&Tails community and a lucky winner would also get an exciting Amazon Gift Voucher. See, we told you, Saving Life have never been this easier. Join hands with us and be the change you want to see in the world. Share this petition with others so that we could save as many lives as possible.

Not every hero wears a cape. Gift a life this Diwali and become a superhero. The Fur&Tails SuperHero batch is earned not given.

• Furandtails.in doesn’t limit this initiative to this Diwali. This would be live all year long on the RESCUE page of our portal.
• It is open to all throughout the year for rescuing injured, lost, stolen and sick dogs and reporting it to our community .
• Provison of free medical assistance, food ,shelter and all such aids to animals, preferably dogs would be available.
• We are operational across 8+cities right now.

So, If you find any dogs in such conditions, Drop a message at @furandtails.in or Call Us at 990501831/9990501832 Remember, We are just a call away.
